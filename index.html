Information about the sunflower flower

The sunflower, also called the sunflower or sunflower, is one of the flowers that follow the asterisks of the astral class. It is so named because it always turns and points toward the sun during its growth period. The seeds of these flowers are known for their high nutritional value and great health benefits.
